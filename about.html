ABOUT ME

I was born and brought up in a tiny little town in India called Vapi. All of my family is doctors but, I was always interested in engineering. I have been making robots since 7th grade and my first functional robot was a nuclear leak sealing robot made out of LEGO Mindstorms. Ever since then, I have been interested about Computer Science and Electronics. In order to pursue this more, I moved to Mumbai for 11th and 12th grade and then decided to move to the US. I moved to Plano, TX in November 2017 and joined Texas A&M University in January 2018.

I love traveling, photgraphy, performing magic, playing racquetball and ping pong.
