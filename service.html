Big Event

With the Aggie Magic Circle

Big Event is the largest one-day, student-run service project in the nation. It is organized by Texas A&M University students.
During the Big Event, I worked with the Aggie Magic Circle to help a farmer in Bryan and College Station community move soil and fertilizers and then to disperse them over the field.
